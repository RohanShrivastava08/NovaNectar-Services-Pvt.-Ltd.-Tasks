ABOUT COMPUTERS




HARDWARE SETUP


Choose your location: Find a clean, well-ventilated spot with enough space for your computer tower, monitor, keyboard, and mouse.


Unpack and assemble (Desktop only): Carefully remove your computer components from their packaging. If you have a desktop, follow the instructions to assemble the tower.


Connect the monitor: Locate the video cable (usually HDMI, DVI, or DisplayPort) and connect it to the monitor and the computer's graphics card (if separate).


Connect the keyboard and mouse: Plug the USB connectors from the keyboard and mouse into the available USB ports on your computer.

SOFTWARE SETUP


Power on: Press the power button on your monitor and computer.
Initial Setup: Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the initial setup process
for your operating system (Windows, macOS, etc.).


This may involve setting your language,
creating a user account, and connecting to Wi-Fi.


Install Updates:
Once the initial setup is complete, update your operating system and
any pre-installed software to ensure you have the latest security patches and features.


Install Applications: Install the applications you need for your work or personal use.
